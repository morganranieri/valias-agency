Context: 
  A user should, after their two month trial of the service, be blocked from usage unless they pay.

Question:
  Should payments go via UI->API->Provider or should they go UI->Provider->API ?

Views:
  In Settings (
    membershipScreen (contains the price, terms, opt-in or out)
    paymentsScreen (add, remove) methods
    wipeAccountScreen (wipe account)
  )

Model: 
  Date user.subscripton-due
  Date user.grace-until (to be clear, not globally inferred)

Controllers: 
  On user signup (
    set user.subscription-due to be the 28th day of this month plus two months,
    set user.grace-until to be the 7th day of this month plus three months,
  )
  On user membership payment (
    upsert user.subscription-due to be the 28th day of the existing user.subscription-due month plus one month;
    upsert user.grace-until to be the 7th day of the updated user.subscription-due month plus one month,
  )


View: 
  If (user.subscription-due is in the past and user.grace-until is not in the past) { 
    show gentleWarningModal (
      informing them that their membership will be deactivated on $user.grace-until with a link to settings/
      don't disappear too easily, consider repeating
      consider triggering a warning email
    )
  }

View: 
  If (user.grace-until is in the past) {
    show blockerModal (
      covers each view (days, dashboard, ins, outs) except settings
      with a link to settings/
    )
  }

Controller: 
  On payment fail (
    email the customer
    retry the payment two days later
    repeat until user.grace-until is past or user turns off their membership 
  )
